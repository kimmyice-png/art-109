Kimberly Layco

Digital Artist

Kimberly Layco is an Asian American artist who lives in the Bay Area in Milpitas. Since Kimberly lives in a very diverse community, she has learned about different cultures and new forms of art. Growing up, Kimberly always watched cartoons and movies that blur the line between reality and cartoons, and this created a sense of longing to create art pieces that make the viewer feel as if they are in another world. While going through mediums of art, she has found the media that immerses the viewer the most is digital media art. She strives to learn about many forms of art and become familiar with each one such as painting, sewing, drawing, and costume design, but right now she is focused on her main medium, 3D art. Currently, she is working with the theme of surreal and fantastical art and wants to dive into the idea of expressing feelings in a constructed reality.
