Kimberly Layco

Digital Artist

What kind of artworks have a magnetic pull that entices the viewer to lean in? I enjoy consuming art that indulges in escapism from reality and has a composition that makes me feel like I am standing inside the piece. I prefer making sceneries that evoke emotions, but I can also create stand-alone 3D parts, photos, or videos. I work mostly in a 3D software called Blender, but I am also familiar with Illustrator, Photoshop, After-Effects, and Lightroom. I also know basic javascript coding. When perusing art, surreal paintings and larger-than-life pieces that contain a secret story or hidden pictures draw me in. This style is what I am working towards in my personal work. .
